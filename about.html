The epic story of Scoreo!

In a laboratory in Russia in 1899 a revolutionary, state of the art, one of a kind, magical website was created to make score keeping for disc golf really easy and more fun. It all started when Abraham Lincoln decided he hated using a lame pencil and piece of paper to keep track of his disc golf score... I know what you're thinking, "the timeline and crucial details aren't even possible!" Oh yea? Yes it is. How? MAGIC! So him and Thomas Jefferson are playing, and Abraham is all like, "Dude, this stupid pencil and paper is lame." Then Thomas Jefferson was all like, "Yo Lincoln, it's almost the twentieth century. There's an app for that." Then Lincoln searched on his Iphone -1, and was like, "Woah, there is no app for that! Well we'll make a website, and then we'll make an app after I get out of this play I have to go to." The rest is history. Sadly, the app never got created.
