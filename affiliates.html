Types of Discs


Distance Drivers

Distance drivers were created to provide players with the maximum distance possible

  • Designed for maximum distance off the tee.
  • Provie the greatest range,speed,glide and control possible.
  • Offered in many different models,plastics for players of every skill.

Fairway Drivers

Fairway Drivers deliver confidence in both driving and approach situations. They provide about the same distance as Distance drivers but with more control and accuracy

  • Easier for beginners
  • Help provide confidence for inexperienced players.

Putt and Approach

Putt and Approach drivers are used for short ranged shots.

  • Best discs for close range shots.
